# Health Insurance Brochure

## Welcome to Our Health Insurance Plans

Thank you for choosing our comprehensive health insurance coverage. This brochure outlines the key features and benefits of our standard health insurance plans.

## Plan Overview

We offer three tiers of coverage to meet your healthcare needs:

### Bronze Plan
- Monthly Premium: $150
- Deductible: $1,500
- Covers 70% of in-network costs
- Out-of-pocket maximum: $5,000
- Prescription drug coverage included

### Silver Plan
- Monthly Premium: $250
- Deductible: $1,000
- Covers 80% of in-network costs
- Out-of-pocket maximum: $4,000
- Prescription drug coverage included
- Vision and dental coverage

### Gold Plan
- Monthly Premium: $400
- Deductible: $500
- Covers 90% of in-network costs
- Out-of-pocket maximum: $2,500
- Comprehensive prescription drug coverage
- Vision, dental, and mental health coverage

## Coverage Details

### Medical Services
- Doctor visits and consultations
- Hospital stays and emergency care
- Preventive care and wellness screenings
- Laboratory and diagnostic tests
- Physical therapy and rehabilitation

### Mental Health Services
- Psychiatry and counseling sessions
- Behavioral health treatment
- Addiction and substance abuse services
- Crisis intervention and support

### Prescription Medications
- Generic medications: $15 copay
- Brand-name medications: $35 copay
- Specialty medications covered up to 80%

### Preventive Care
All plans cover preventive services at no cost, including:
- Annual physical exams
- Immunizations
- Cancer screenings
- Cholesterol and blood pressure checks
- Mental health assessments

## How to Use Your Benefits

### Finding In-Network Providers
Visit our provider directory online or call our member services team to find participating doctors, hospitals, and specialists in your area.

### Submitting Claims
Claims should be submitted within 30 days of service. Most claims are processed within 10 business days.

### Emergency Care
In case of a medical emergency, proceed to the nearest emergency room or call 911. Emergency services are covered at 100% with no copay.

## Exclusions and Limitations

The following services are not covered under our plans:
- Cosmetic procedures
- Experimental treatments not approved by the FDA
- Services received outside the United States
- Non-prescription medications and supplements
- Treatment related to workplace injuries

## Network Requirements

To maximize your benefits, use in-network providers:
- In-network: 80-90% coverage
- Out-of-network: 50-60% coverage

We maintain a broad network of over 50,000 healthcare providers nationwide.

## Member Benefits and Extras

- 24/7 nurse hotline for medical advice
- Wellness programs and fitness discounts
- Mental health support resources
- Preventive care at no additional cost
- Online portal for managing your account
